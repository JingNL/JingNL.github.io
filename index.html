Slider Game

Things to Know:

  • The puzzle will already be in a solved state when first loading the game. You will have to shuffle the puzzle yourself.
  • After shuffling, you can click the start button to begin counting your moves.
  • Clicking the reset button will stop counting your moves and clicking start again will reset the counter to 0.
  • Your goal is to get the puzzle into the initial solved state.
  • You can click a block next to the empty space in order to move the block to that space.

    • Number of Moves: 0
