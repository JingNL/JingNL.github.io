About Me

My name is Jing Nang. I'm 17 years old. My house is in the northeast section of Queens, where all the housing is, and I am not married. I enrolled as a student for Brooklyn Tech, and I get home every day by 5 PM at the latest. I don't smoke, but I occasionally drink water. I'm in bed by 11 PM, and make sure I get eight hours of sleep, no matter what. After brushing my teeth and looking twenty minutes at my phone before going to bed, I usually have no problems sleeping until morning. Just like a baby, I wake up without any fatigue or stress in the morning.

Despite my name I do not understand a lick of Mandarin even though I also took three years of Mandarin in Middle School. I was born and have lived in Queens my whole life but I have moved houses from time to time. I have flew out on an airplane to Florida but I have not been out of the country. In my lifetime I have had a few pet fish, a pet turtle, and pet rabbits. I also like animals as long as they're not trying to kill me.
